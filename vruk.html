Community & Training

One of the core values of VRUK is to teach and train. Songteach, their online community site, gives worship leaders around the world access to tutorials and music to help them mature and grow in their craft. Content is protected from illegal distribution by requiring a free membership.

One if the key features of the site runs behind the scenes, users can upload video content and the site automatically encodes it into the correct size and format, saving VRUK staff time with every upload.
